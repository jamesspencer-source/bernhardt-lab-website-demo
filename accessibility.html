Accessibility

Accessibility statement

The Thomas Bernhardt Lab website is designed to align with Harvard Medical School web guidance and accessibility expectations.

Our current standards

We target WCAG 2.1 Level AA conformance, consistent with Harvard Medical School digital accessibility guidance.

The site includes keyboard-focus indicators, skip links, semantic headings, alt text for content images, labeled form fields, accessible iframe titles, and reduced-motion support.

Auto-rotating image sections include previous/next controls plus a pause control, and embedded video is configured with captions plus a transcript path on the source platform.

Third-party embeds (for example, Google Maps and YouTube) are provided through external platforms and may have platform-specific accessibility behavior.
