Research Interest

I am interested in studying the regulation of peptidoglycan hydrolysis during cell division in Escherichia coli. Peptidoglycan synthesized and deposited at the division site has to be surgically cleaved in a regulated and coordinated fashion for proper cell division. My current focus is on revealing the molecular mechanisms that control the hydrolases and their activators to mediate proper PG cleavage.
