Team Profile

James Spencer

Research Operations Leader | Laboratory Manager, HHMI Investigator Laboratories (Bernhardt & Abraham Labs)

Lab dates: 2019-present

I lead research operations for the Bernhardt and Abraham HHMI Investigator laboratories at Harvard Medical School, supporting shared BSL-2 space across two buildings. My work spans budgeting, hiring, procurement, compliance (COMS/IACUC), and equipment/facilities planning. I also lead operations for the HMS Community Phages internship and chair HHMI's Lab Management Network of Professionals (LMNOP) Advisory Board.

Back to team directory Contact LinkedIn GitHub

Research Interest

I am motivated by the urgent global threat of antimicrobial resistance and by the possibility that foundational microbiology can help change that trajectory. While my role is operational rather than bench-based, I view research operations as a direct engine of discovery: by building reliable systems for staffing, compliance, procurement, budgeting, and infrastructure, I help scientists spend more time on the experiments that matter most. I care deeply about supporting rigorous, high-impact science that advances understanding of bacterial cell envelope biology and informs future antibiotic strategies. For me, this work is about multiplying the productivity of exceptional researchers and contributing, upstream but meaningfully, to efforts that reduce human disease and suffering from drug-resistant infections, both now and for future generations.
